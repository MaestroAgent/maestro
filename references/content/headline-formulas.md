# Headline Formulas Reference

## The 10 Core Headline Archetypes

### 1. The List
**Formula:** [Number] [Things] to [Achieve Desired Outcome]

- 7 SEO Mistakes Killing Your Organic Traffic
- 12 Email Subject Lines That Get 40%+ Open Rates
- 5 Pricing Strategies That Doubled Our Revenue

**When to use:** Educational content, blog posts, social media. Always odd numbers or specific numbers (not round).

### 2. The How-To
**Formula:** How to [Achieve Specific Result] [Without Pain / In Timeframe]

- How to Write a Landing Page That Converts at 10%
- How to Build an Email List Without Paid Ads
- How to Run a Marketing Team With AI (and 2 People)

**When to use:** Tutorials, guides, search-optimized content.

### 3. The Question
**Formula:** [Question that implies a problem or opportunity]

- Are You Leaving Revenue on Your Pricing Page?
- Why Are Your Best Customers Churning?
- What If Your Entire Marketing Team Was AI?

**When to use:** When you want to trigger self-reflection. Works well in emails and social.

### 4. The Contrarian
**Formula:** Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong / [Contrarian Claim]

- Why Content Marketing Is Dead (And What Replaced It)
- Stop A/B Testing Your Landing Pages
- Your SEO Strategy Is Backwards — Here's Why

**When to use:** Thought leadership, social media, newsletters. Creates debate and sharing.

### 5. The Story
**Formula:** How [Person/I] [Achieved Result] [with Specific Detail]

- How a Solo Founder Hit $1M ARR With Zero Paid Ads
- I Replaced My Marketing Team With AI — Here's What Happened
- The Email That Generated $50K in Revenue (Full Breakdown)

**When to use:** Case studies, personal content, YouTube. Stories are the most shareable format.

### 6. The Comparison
**Formula:** [Thing A] vs [Thing B]: [What You Need to Know]

- SEO vs Paid Ads: Where to Invest Your First $10K
- ConvertKit vs Mailchimp: The Honest Comparison
- In-House vs Agency: Which Marketing Model Wins?

**When to use:** Commercial intent content, SEO, decision-stage buyers.

### 7. The Ultimate Guide
**Formula:** The [Complete/Ultimate/Definitive] Guide to [Topic]

- The Complete Guide to SaaS Pricing in 2026
- Everything You Need to Know About Email Deliverability
- The Founder's Guide to YouTube Marketing

**When to use:** Pillar content, SEO cornerstone pages. These are long, comprehensive pieces.

### 8. The Mistake/Warning
**Formula:** [Number] [Topic] Mistakes [Audience] Make (And How to Fix Them)

- 7 Onboarding Mistakes That Kill Your Trial Conversion
- The #1 Landing Page Error I See on Every SaaS Site
- 3 Pricing Page Mistakes Costing You Revenue Right Now

**When to use:** Problem-aware audiences. Creates urgency ("am I making these mistakes?").

### 9. The Direct Benefit
**Formula:** Get [Specific Result] [In Timeframe / Without Pain]

- Double Your Email Open Rates in 30 Days
- Get 10X More Organic Traffic Without Writing More Content
- Save 20 Hours/Week With These Marketing Automations

**When to use:** Ads, landing pages, CTAs. Most direct and conversion-focused.

### 10. The Curiosity Gap
**Formula:** [Interesting Setup] ... [Incomplete Information]

- The Marketing Channel Nobody Is Talking About
- What Happened When I Paused All Paid Ads for 90 Days
- The One Metric That Actually Predicts Churn

**When to use:** Email subject lines, social posts, YouTube titles. Requires the content to actually deliver.

## Headline Power-Ups

### Specificity Boosts
- Bad: "How to Grow Your Business"
- Better: "How to Grow Your SaaS to $1M ARR"
- Best: "How a Solo Founder Grew to $1M ARR in 18 Months With Zero Paid Ads"

### Audience Targeting
- Add "for [specific audience]" to any headline
- "Email Marketing for SaaS Founders"
- "The CMO's Guide to AI-Powered Marketing"

### Timeframe Addition
- Add "in [specific timeframe]"
- "Build a 10K Email List in 90 Days"
- "Launch Your First Ad Campaign This Weekend"

### Credibility Injection
- Add data, results, or authority
- "[Based on 10,000 A/B Tests]"
- "[What $50M in Ad Spend Taught Us]"

## Platform-Specific Title Rules

### Blog / SEO
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Under 60 characters (shows fully in Google)
- Front-load the keyword
- Match search intent

### YouTube
- Under 60 characters
- Curiosity + specificity
- Capitalize Key Words (YouTube convention)
- Test titles after publishing (change within 48 hours based on CTR)

### Email Subject Lines
- Under 50 characters (mobile)
- Personal tone works better than professional
- Questions outperform statements
- Emojis: test, but don't rely on them

### Social Media
- Hook in first line (before "see more" truncation)
- Bold/contrarian performs best
- Tag relevant people/brands for amplification

## A/B Testing Headlines

### What to Test
1. Emotional angle (fear vs aspiration vs curiosity)
2. Specificity level (vague vs ultra-specific)
3. Format (question vs statement vs list)
4. Length (short punchy vs long descriptive)

### Sample Size
- Email: 1,000+ per variant minimum
- Social: 48 hours of organic reach
- Ads: 1,000+ impressions per variant
- Blog: Use Google Search Console CTR data over 2+ weeks

### Decision Framework
- Statistical significance: 95% confidence minimum
- Practical significance: >10% difference to be meaningful
- Don't over-optimize: sometimes the second-best headline has broader appeal
