# Email Templates Reference

## Welcome Sequence Templates

### Email 1: Deliver + Set Expectations
```
Subject: Here's your [lead magnet name]

Hey [name],

Welcome — and thanks for grabbing [lead magnet].

Here's your download: [link]

Over the next [timeframe], I'll send you [what to expect].
Each one is [short/actionable/etc.].

Quick question to kick things off:
What's your biggest challenge with [topic] right now?

Just hit reply — I read every response.

[Name]
```

### Email 2: Story + Credibility
```
Subject: Why I [built this / started this / care about this]

[Name],

Quick backstory on why I'm so obsessed with [topic]:

[2-3 paragraph personal story — struggle, turning point, result]

That experience is why I [created product / built this resource].

Tomorrow I'll share [preview of next email value].

[Name]
```

### Email 3: Quick Win
```
Subject: Try this [today/this week] — takes 5 minutes

[Name],

Here's something you can do right now that [specific benefit]:

[Step 1]
[Step 2]
[Step 3]

That's it. Most people see [result] within [timeframe].

[Name]

P.S. If you tried it, reply and tell me how it went.
```

## Formatting Guidelines

### Structure Rules
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- One idea per paragraph
- White space between paragraphs
- Bold key phrases sparingly
- One primary CTA per email
- P.S. for secondary message or urgency

### Subject Line Rules
- Under 50 characters (mobile optimization)
- No ALL CAPS
- No excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Avoid spam triggers: "free", "act now", "limited time" (in excess)
- Personalization works: [name] or relevant detail
- A/B test every time

### Preview Text
- Complements subject line (doesn't repeat it)
- Creates additional curiosity
- Under 90 characters
- If you don't set it, email clients pull first line of body

### Mobile Optimization
- 50-60% of opens are mobile
- Single column layout
- Large tap targets for links/buttons
- Preview in mobile before sending
